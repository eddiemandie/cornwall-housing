# Priced Out of Cornwall?

**Portfolio Project 01 — Edgar M.**

## Live report

[→ View the full case study](https://eddiemandie.github.io/cornwall-housing/insight_summary.html)

## What this project does

Analyses 399,805 residential property transactions in Cornwall from 1995 to 2026 using HM Land Registry Price Paid Data — asking how housing affordability has changed over 30 years, which areas have been hit hardest, and what drove the two biggest price surges in the data.

Key finding: Cornwall house prices rose 484% between 1995 and 2025, against cumulative inflation of 169% and nominal wage growth of 150%. Prices grew nearly three times faster than inflation and more than three times faster than wages — turning what was a manageable stretch for local buyers into a structural affordability crisis.
