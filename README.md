# Priced Out of Cornwall?
### Analysing 30 years of housing affordability, 1995–present

## The question
How has housing affordability in Cornwall changed since 1995, and what's driving it?

## Questions and hypotheses

| Question | Hypothesis |
|----------|------------|
| How have prices changed by area and property type? | Coastal towns like Falmouth and Penzance have risen faster than inland areas like Redruth |
| What premium do new-build homes carry? | New-builds cost 15-25% more than existing homes throughout the period |
| Are there seasonal patterns in transactions? | Sales peak in summer reflecting the holiday and second-home market |
| How does Cornwall compare to the England average? | Cornwall has grown faster than the national average since 2010 |
| How stretched are local buyers against local earnings? | The price-to-earnings ratio has at least doubled since 1995 |

## Data source

**HM Land Registry — Price Paid Data**
Every residential property sale in England and Wales since 1995.
Download: https://www.gov.uk/government/statistical-data-sets/price-paid-data-downloads
